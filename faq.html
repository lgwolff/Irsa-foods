Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Simply add products to your cart from the homepage, proceed to checkout, fill in your delivery details, and submit the order. You will receive an order confirmation once your order is placed.
What payment methods do you offer?
We operate on Cash on Delivery (COD). An upfront delivery confirmation fee is required to confirm your order, and the remaining amount is payable upon delivery.
What is the upfront fee?
The upfront fee is a delivery confirmation charge. It does not reduce your product price and helps us ensure successful deliveries.
